Who We Are

About Us

PDcars is the leading independent car buying consultancy. We inspect, negotiate, and handle all the details so you get the perfect car with total confidence.

Unbiased Advice, Expert Diagnostics

At PDcars, we do things differently. We are not a dealership, and we don't hold any car inventory. This means our advice is 100% unbiased. Our sole mission is to help you buy, sell, or maintain your vehicle with absolute peace of mind.

Founded in Nagpur and recently expanded to Pune, our team consists of experienced diagnostic technicians, expert price negotiators, and customer relationship professionals who guide you from inspection to final registration.

Our Mission

To eliminate the stress, opacity, and hidden risks from the car-buying process, ensuring that every buyer turns the key with complete transparency.

We work exclusively for you, checking every vehicle's mechanical health, clear titles, and fair market price.
